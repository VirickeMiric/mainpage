This is my first post I am writing. I decided to do an environmental module, because it is really an essential part of my life. It might sound strange, but it is exactly the reason why I did not continue to work on this field, I wanted to experience the magic in our world (nature) just as it is without the stress that normally comes with a workplace.

By sharing (hopefully) valuable information with the world , the few people that read my blog I believe I contribute to the utopia I believe in.

Why do I believe it holds significance?

I recall a quote that resonated with me, and many said before me:

"I can't single-handedly alter the course of the world."

(A sentiment echoed by almost 8 billion individuals across the globe.)
